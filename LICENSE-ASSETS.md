# Usage Terms

These assets are provided by GameDirection for building or updating materials that represent GameDirection or a GameDirection project (websites, press coverage, partner pages, storefronts).

## Do

- Use the logo files as provided in `brand/logo/`
- Use `#00ACED` as the accent/brand color
- Use Raleway as the typeface for brand surfaces
- Keep clearspace around the logo (see `brand/guide/brand-guidelines.md`)
- Credit "GameDirection" when featuring a project case study

## Don't

- Don't recolor the logo mark to a color other than brand blue, black, or white
- Don't place the blue mark on a low-contrast background
- Don't stretch, skew, rotate, or add unapproved effects (drop shadows, outlines, gradients) to the logo
- Don't crop into the logo's clearspace
- Don't substitute a different font for brand surfaces (headings, buttons, logotype-adjacent text)
- Don't imply endorsement of a third-party product beyond what was actually contracted/worked on

Full visual rules: [`brand/guide/brand-guidelines.md`](brand/guide/brand-guidelines.md)
