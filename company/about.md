# About GameDirection

GameDirection is a game studio focused on memorable player experiences, accessibility-minded design, and support for neurodivergent players.

> "Hello! I am a Cleveland game developer who believes in creating memorable experiences through accessibility design for neurodivergent players."

## Mission

The studio's goal is to become self-sustaining so it can support artists and collaborators doing strong creative work.

## Focus Areas

- Game design
- Graphic design
- Visual-based programming
- Accessible play experiences
